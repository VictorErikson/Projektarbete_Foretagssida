How it all started

The Nature Collective was founded in 2015 by a group of passionate outdoor enthusiasts who shared a common vision: to make nature experiences accessible to everyone. After years of exploring the Swedish mountains, forests, and lakes, the founders realized there was a lack of a dedicated platform where adventure-seekers could find high-quality products that would truly enhance their outdoor experiences. With a strong belief in sustainability and a deep respect for nature, they began collaborating with local artisans and manufacturers to create a range of outdoor products that were not only functional but also environmentally friendly. The Nature Collective launched its first website in 2016, and after receiving positive feedback from customers, the company quickly grew. Today, The Nature Collective offers a wide selection of products, from high-quality tents and sleeping bags to clothing and gear for all types of outdoor activities. The company is also committed to educating and inspiring its customers through blogs and guides on outdoor living. With a passion for adventure and a strong connection to nature, The Nature Collective continues to be a leading player in the outdoor product market in Sweden.
