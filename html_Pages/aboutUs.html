How it all started

The Nature Collective was founded in 2015 by a group of outdoor enthusiasts who wanted to make nature experiences accessible to everyone. After years of exploring Sweden’s mountains, forests, and lakes, they noticed the need for a platform offering high-quality, sustainable outdoor products. With a strong belief in sustainability, they partnered with local artisans and manufacturers to create environmentally friendly gear.

In 2016, they launched their first website, which receivedpositive feedback and led to rapid growth. Today, The NatureCollective offers a diverse range of products, including tents, sleeping bags, clothing, and accessories, along with educational blogs and guides to inspire outdoor living. The company remains a leading name in Sweden’s outdoor product market, driven by a passion for adventure and a commitment to nature.
